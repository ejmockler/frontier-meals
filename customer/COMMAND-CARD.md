**For customers** — Pinned in Telegram bot on `/start`

## Your Commands at a Glance

Here's what you can do with the Frontier Meals bot:

### `/start`
Begin onboarding or reconnect your account

### `/diet`
Update your dietary preferences (Vegan, Vegetarian, Pescatarian, or Everything)

### `/skip`
Skip meal dates for upcoming weeks (you can skip weeks after the current one)

### `/undo`
Undo your last skip

### `/status`
View your upcoming meals and active skips

### `/billing`
Manage your subscription and payment method

### `/help`
Show this command list and get support
